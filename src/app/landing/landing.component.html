About Us

Telcotec is a company with nearly twenty years of experience in the technical and management fields of telecommunications. Our engineers / consultants have managed and optimized several fixed and mobile networks with several million subscribers and conducted dozens of multi-million euro study and infrastructure projects. They have carried out numerous audit, study, optimization, consulting and training assignments in their areas of expertise. We are committed to providing high quality professional services in engineering, consulting and training in the technical and management fields of telecommunications to operators, regulators, telecommunications equipment manufacturers and large corporations. Our goal is to make us useful to you by understanding your specificities and knowing your constraints.
